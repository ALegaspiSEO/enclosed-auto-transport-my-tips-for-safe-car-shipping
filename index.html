Types of Enclosed Carriers

I was surprised to learn there are different types of enclosed carriers:

  • Soft-Side Carriers: Vinyl tarps protect from weather but may let some dust in. They’re cheaper but still safe.
  • Hard-Side Carriers: Solid walls offer maximum protection. They’re pricier but ideal for delicate cars like mine.
  • Liftgate Carriers: Use liftgates for safe loading, great for heavy or low-clearance vehicles.

I chose a hard-side carrier for the ultimate security.
